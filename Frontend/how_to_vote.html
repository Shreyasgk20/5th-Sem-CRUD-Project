How to Vote

Step-by-step guide to cast your vote securely

Voting Process Overview

The online voting system ensures secure, transparent, and accessible voting for all eligible citizens. Follow these simple steps to cast your vote.

1

Registration

If you are not already registered as a voter, complete the voter registration process by providing your personal details, address, and contact information.

Register Now
2

Account Verification

After registration, your account will be verified by the election authorities. You will receive confirmation once your account is approved for voting.

Note: Only verified voters can participate in the voting process.
3

Login to Vote

On the voting day, log in using your Voter ID and Date of Birth. Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

Login to Vote
4

Review Candidates

Carefully review all candidates and their manifestos. Read their party information and policy statements before making your choice.

View All Candidates
5

Cast Your Vote

Select your preferred candidate by clicking the radio button next to their name. Review your selection carefully before confirming.

Important: You can only vote once. Your vote is final and cannot be changed after submission.
6

Confirmation

After casting your vote, you will receive a confirmation message. Your vote has been securely recorded in the system.

Security: All votes are encrypted and stored securely. Your identity remains confidential.

Important Information

Security Features

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure authentication
  • One vote per voter
  • Anonymous voting

Voting Requirements

  • Valid Voter ID (EPIC)
  • Verified account
  • Stable internet connection
  • Valid date of birth

Support

  • Helpline: 1800-123-4321
  • Email: support@electionseva.gov.in
  • Available 24/7 during voting period
  • Technical assistance provided
